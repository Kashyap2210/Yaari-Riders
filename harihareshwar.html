Harihareshwar Ride: The One With The Views.

  • Our journey began at 5 am from Oberoi Mall, with Shubham riding with one pillion. We opted to have breakfast after sunrise. Harihareshwar, a tranquil coastal village in Maharashtra, awaited us.
  • Despite the early start, we reached Harihareshwar by 1 pm. After darshan at the Mahadev Temple, we explored the beach nestled within a valley, enjoying the mesmerizing rock formations shaped by the sea waves. We relaxed, took photos, and indulged in the serene atmosphere. After climbing back up, we refreshed with nimbu pani and impulsively booked a RO-RO ferry from Alibag to Mumbai. Feeling fatigued, we agreed that one-day rides weren't ideal. Despite this, we reached the ferry early, enjoying the moonlit sea views on the top level. Following dinner in Goregaon West, we parted ways, concluding our ride.
  • We also decided to uphold a tradition of visiting a Mahadev temple on every ride, a tradition we've continued to follow since.
    During the ride, we encountered numerous breathtaking views and picturesque river crossings. We unanimously agree that this journey stands out as one of the most scenic rides we've experienced to date.
