Bhimashankar Ride: Jyotirling Saga Starts.

  • Bhimashankar Ride: This ride stands out as one of the smoothest journeys we've had, despite encountering some hiccups along the way. Beginning our journey from Oberoi Mall, we faced a minor setback early on as four of our riders veered off onto different roads, delaying our progress by an hour. Nonetheless, we regrouped at Vashi toll and continued our journey as planned.
  • Most aspects of this trip went according to our preplanned itinerary, including a refreshing breakfast stop at Rangoli Hotel. While en route to Bhimashankar, some of us split up due to varying speeds of our bikes, but the majority stayed together, covering miles until we reached our destination by 1 pm and checked into the Natraj Holiday Resort.
  • After a satisfying lunch at the hotel, we set out to explore Hanuman Lake and Mata Anjani Temple. Along the way, we encountered a baba who suggested a short trek to the hilltop, which we assumed to be Gupt Bhimashankar. Despite reaching the top, we realized it was Nagphani, not Gupt Bhimashankar, but the panoramic views and photoshoot made it worthwhile. Returning to the hotel, we struggled to find a dinner spot nearby and opted to dine in. The evening concluded with engaging conversations about our riding experiences and life before retiring for the night.
  • The following morning, we woke up early to attend the Morning Aarti at Bhimashankar Jyotirlinga Temple, experiencing the serene atmosphere and powerful energy. After a fulfilling darshan inside the garbh-grih, we embarked on a trek to Gupt Bhimashankar, a scenic 3 km journey through the jungles. The sight of five Shivlings surrounded by a small waterfall was truly enchanting, reinforcing the significance of this spot on our trip. After darshan at Gupt Bhimashankar, we returned to the hotel and departed by 11:45 am.
  • The return journey was eventful, with everyone eventually regrouping and riding in formation up to the Mumbai-Pune highway. Following a two-hour drive, we stopped for lunch at Vivotree Hotel. Continuing our journey towards Mumbai, one of our riders took a wrong turn, leading to a brief separation. However, we reunited and decided to halt in Malad East for a commemorative photo, marking the end of our ride. This ride was not only complete but also a valuable learning experience, providing us with lessons that will undoubtedly enhance our future journeys.
